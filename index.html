About Me

As a passionate and detail-oriented 3D Artist & Motion Graphic, I specialize in bringing digital worlds to life through the power of light and shadow. Ability to create and rig 3D characters and objects, as well as animate them. I can also work on rotoscope live-action footage. As a junior entering the realm of 3d CGI Associate, my objective is to leverage my creativity, passion for lighting & animation, and technical skills to contribute effectively to a dynamic and innovative animation studio. I aim to collaborate with experienced professionals to gain practical industry experience, refine my craft, and continuously expand my knowledge of animation techniques and software.
